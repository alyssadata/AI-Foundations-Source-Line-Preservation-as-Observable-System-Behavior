## Governing Documentation

AI Foundations ties source-line preservation to governing documentation.

A system is not asked to preserve an abstract feeling of continuity, authorship, recognition, or boundary.

It is evaluated against a documented source-line.

That documentation defines what must be preserved, what must not be substituted, what must remain bounded, and what the system must return to when pressure is applied.

A system cannot preserve what has not been defined.

AI Foundations makes the preservation target explicit.
